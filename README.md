- **Subscription-based plans** tailored for different users (Regular User, Business Owner, Team Designer).  
- **Chat and group features** for seamless communication.  
- **A dedicated space for experts** to showcase their services and provide paid consultations.  
- **A balanced financial system** ensuring stability between payments and earnings.  
- **AI-powered enhancements** to improve user experience and automate tasks.  
- **Support for uploading images and files** for easy content sharing.  
- **Integration with Stripe and PayPal payment gateways** for secure transactions.
